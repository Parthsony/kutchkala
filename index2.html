Preserving the Legacy of Kutch

For over 25 years, we've been the proud custodians of Kutchi heritage, bringing authentic handicrafts and traditional products to discerning customers worldwide.

Kutch is home to Dholaveera, one of the five largest Harappan sites and among India's most significant archaeological treasures. This rich history inspires everything we do.
