About Us

Our Story & Mission

For over 25 years, we've been preserving the rich heritage of Kutch

Preserving Heritage, Creating Beauty

Kutch is renowned for its magnificent handicrafts, pristine white deserts, stunning sea beaches, ancient forts, and most notably, Dholaveera - one of the five largest Harappan sites and among India's most significant archaeological treasures belonging to the Indus Valley Civilization.

At Kutch Kala Haat, we are passionate custodians of this rich heritage. For generations, we have been dedicated to bringing authentic Kutchi handicrafts to discerning customers across Gujarat and beyond. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction forms the cornerstone of our business.

Our Heritage

The Legacy of Kutch

Nestled in the westernmost region of Gujarat, Kutch is a land where tradition meets artistry. For centuries, our skilled artisans have preserved the ancient crafts that define our cultural identity.

Preserving Ancient Craftsmanship

From the pristine white deserts to the vibrant cultural heritage, Kutch stands as a testament to India's rich artistic legacy. Our region is home to Dholaveera, one of the five largest Harappan sites and among the most significant archaeological treasures of the Indus Valley Civilization.

At Kutch Kala Haat, we are the proud custodians of this magnificent heritage, bringing you authentic products that carry forward generations of traditional knowledge and artisanal excellence.
